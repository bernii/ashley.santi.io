Traveling around Argentina

Since you're coming all this way to party with us, we hope you'll consider taking some time to explore Argentina (or its surrounding countries) before or after the wedding! From gigantic waterwalls in Iguazu, to drinking delicious Malbec wines in Mendoza, to standing in the southernmost city in the world, Ushuaia, there are countless awesome things to see and do in South America.

We asked our travel agent to put together some sample iteneraries to give you an idea of trips to take. Please keep in mind these prices are guestimates based on 4 star hotels (3 star equivalent in the US) - you can easily go way less expensive (or more) if you'd like!

Ushuaia

The southernmost city in the world, referred to as "El fin del mundo," Ushuaia has lots of national parks, a small touristy town, and great natural beauty.
  • Roundtrip flights from Buenos Aires
  • 3 nights at Hotel Albatros
  • Roundtrip transportation from Ushuaia airport to the hotel
  • Excursion to Parque Nacional Tierra del Fuego
  • $968 USD / person

Calafate

Located in Patagonia, Calafate is the starting point to visit the Perito Moreno Glacier (the world's third largest reserve of fresh water) and the Fitz Roy Mountain.
  • Roundtrip flights from Buenos Aires
  • 3 nights at Hotel Mirador del Lago Sector superior
  • Roundtrip transportation from Calafate airport to the hotel
  • Excursion to Glaciar Perito Moreno
  • $880 USD / person

Mendoza

The fourth largest city in Argentina, Mendoza is a popular tourist destination, know for its wineries and olive oil making.
  • Roundtrip flights from Buenos Aires
  • 3 nights at Hotel Amerian Executive Categoria Premier
  • Roundtrip transportation from Mendoza airport to the hotel
  • Excursion to visit the city and surroundings
  • $698 USD / person

Iguazu

Iguazu is home to one of the world's largest waterfalls. Surrounded by national parks, there are also subtropical rainforests to explore within the falls.
  • Roundtrip flights from Buenos Aires
  • 3 nights at Hotel IGUAZU GRAND HOTEL o LOI SUITES IGUAZU HOTEL
  • Roundtrip transportation from Iguazu airport to the hotel
  • Excursion to Iguazu Falls
  • $950 USD / person
